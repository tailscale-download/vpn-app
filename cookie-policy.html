Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Giperscale uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website and use our services. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

1. What Are Cookies

1.1 Definition

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

1.2 How They Work

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Giperscale) are called "first-party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies".

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They include:

  • Authentication cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Session management cookies

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve our site's performance. They help us know which pages are the most and least popular.

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as:

  • Language preferences
  • Region settings
  • User interface customization

3. Cookie Usage

3.1 Primary Purposes

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Improving our services
  • Providing personalized experiences
  • Ensuring website security

3.2 Analytics and Performance

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve our services and user experience.

4. Cookie Control

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Common browser settings include:

  • Accepting all cookies
  • Rejecting all cookies
  • Notifying you when a cookie is set

4.2 Managing Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent manager or your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Cookies

5.1 Partner Services

We work with partners who may also set cookies on our website for:

  • Analytics services
  • Security features
  • Performance monitoring

5.2 Third-Party Privacy Policies

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and may use cookies differently.

6. Cookie Updates

6.1 Policy Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as we introduce new features or change how we use cookies.

6.2 Notification

We will notify you of any significant changes to this policy.
